Build With Us

We're hiring people who ship fast, think clearly, and care about small businesses.

Who we are: Remodely AI is building AI-powered software for small businesses — starting with the trades. We're a bootstrapped startup in Phoenix, Arizona. Remote-first. Small team. Big ambition. Founded by someone from the trades who learned to code.

Why Remodely AI

  • Real impact: Our customers are electricians, plumbers, and remodelers — people who build things with their hands. The software we make directly changes how they run their businesses.
  • Early stage: You're not employee #500. You're joining when the foundation is being laid. Your work will shape the product, the culture, and the company.
  • AI-native: We build with OpenAI, Anthropic, ElevenLabs, and Twilio. AI isn't a feature — it's the core of everything we do.
  • Equity: Early team members get meaningful ownership (0.5%–2%).
  • Remote-first: Work from anywhere. We care about output, not office hours.
  • No bureaucracy: Small team, fast decisions, direct access to the founder.
